Our Mission

CAFALL Charity is a nonprofit organization dedicated to making a positive impact, particularly in Africa. We aim to improve the lives of those in need by providing access to essential resources, education, sports, and more. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to live a healthy, fulfilling life, and we are committed to making this vision a reality.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate individuals united by a shared goal: to make a meaningful difference in the lives of others. With dedication and compassion, we strive to enhance the well-being of individuals and communities. We collaborate with a network of partners and supporters who share our vision and values. Together, we leverage collective strengths and resources to bring about positive change.

How You Can Help

Your support is crucial to our success. Whether through donations, volunteer work, or partnerships, every contribution helps us make a lasting impact. We invite you to join us in our efforts to create a better world for all.

Our Vision

At CAFALL Charity, we envision a world where everyone has the opportunity to live a healthy and fulfilling life. We believe that addressing environmental and social challenges is key to creating positive change and improving lives globally.

Our vision is fueled by our unwavering commitment to our mission: to enhance the well-being of those in need and foster lasting change. Through collaboration and collective action, we strive to create a brighter future for individuals and communities alike.

We recognize the power of collective impact and believe that everyone has a role to play in making a difference. Join us on this journey toward a better world, where hope, opportunity, and prosperity are accessible to all. Together, we can shape a future filled with resilience and leave a lasting legacy.

Our Goals

Support

Provide school supplies to families in need, ensuring children have essential tools for learning.

Outreach

Distribute basketball shoes and sports equipment to underserved individuals, promoting physical activity and well-being.

Education Initiatives

Support educational programs in Africa, focusing on building schools, providing materials, and fostering literacy in Senegal.

Access

Offer medical support to underserved communities, improving access to vital services and health outcomes.

Stewardship

Advocate for environmental protection and sustainable practices to reduce waste and combat climate change.

Justice and Equality

Champion social justice, advocate for marginalized groups, and combat discrimination to create a more inclusive society.

Our Impact

First Backpacks Giveaway

2018

CAFALL Charity held its inaugural backpack giveaway, providing essential school supplies to children in need. This initiative equipped students with the tools necessary for learning and alleviated financial burdens on families. The backpacks were warmly received, instilling a sense of hope and empowerment.

Shoes Giveaway at SEBI Basket Club

CAFALL Charity teamed up with Sebi Basket Club to distribute basketball shoes to underserved individuals in Senegal. This initiative promoted physical activity, enhanced well-being, and inspired a sense of community among recipients.

Shoes Giveaway at POUT Basket Club

CAFALL Charity partnered with Pout Basket Club to organize a basketball shoe giveaway. This initiative provided new and gently used basketball shoes to underserved individuals, enabling them to participate in sports and fostering a sense of community through sports.
